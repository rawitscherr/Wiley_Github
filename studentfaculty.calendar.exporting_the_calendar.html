Exporting the Calendar

Sometimes, students and faculty wish to export Engage calendar events so that they may be used by other calendar management applications.

This document illustrates how to export the calendar.

  1. Locate either the Weekly Calendar block or the Calendar block and click on the month hyperlink to load a full view of the calendar.

  2. Click Export Calendar at the bottom of the page.

  3. Use the radio buttons to either export All events or Events related to courses, which does not include personal uesr-specific events.

    Then, choose the timeframe of events to be exported.

  4. Buttons at the bottom of the page allow you to choose the export format as described below.

    1. Get calendar URL: Provides a link that you can send to yourself or others. Clicking this link will add the calendar and events to an Outlook calendar.

    2. Export: Generates an iCal export file. Once the file is opened, it adds the calendar events to your Outlook calendar.

    3. Orange iCal button: Creates an iCal export file with all of your calendar data.
