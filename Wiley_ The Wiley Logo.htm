The Wiley Logo

The Wiley logo is a symbol of over 200 years of providing high quality content and the most prominent expression of our brand. It is therefore vitally important that to use it correctly and consistently.

Clear Space and Sizing

EPS wordmark files may be scaled to any size necessary for applications, as long as the minimum size requirements are met. Always maintain the aspect ratio when scaling.

Minimum size refers to the smallest allowable wordmark size. The wordmark is available in one size that can be scaled down to a minimum size of 0.4 inch (10 mm) in width for print usage or 70px on digital channels.

Clear space should always be maintained around the wordmark. It defines the minimum distance to any other type or graphic element, as well as to the edge of a printed piece. The minimum clear space is equal to the height of the "Y" in the diagram below.

Wiley Logo Usage—DON'Ts

The logo may not be re-created or altered in any way. There are two approved versions of the logo: black and white. Do not alter the logo or change its color.
